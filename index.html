Resume/CV

Aiqi (Chelsea) Cui '20 is a double Information Systems and Stats & Machine Learning major at Carnegie Mellon University. At Carnegie Mellon, Aiqi is the co-founder and head of Talent Pool in CMU Summit on US-China Innovation and Entrepreneurship, a mentor in Strong Women Strong Girls, and a member in Women's Ultimate Frisbee.

Aiqi's great passion is in creating things that make people feel "wow". Her academic interests lie in the areas splitting between Data Science/Machine Learning and Full-Stack Development.
